Intermediate Level Chess Coaching

Intermediate chess training is for rated players between 1000 to 1400. This certified chess courses helps in preparation for chess tournaments which includes deep study of opening, middlegame and endgame. It is recommended to go to the next level of professional chess.

Advanced Level Chess Coaching

Advanced chess training is for players with a specific target in chess such as to qualify for a District, Division, State or National level chess championships or to become a titleholder such as Candidate Master (CM), FIDE Master (FM), International Master (IM) and Grand Master (GM) in chess tournaments.
